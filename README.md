# 🧜‍♀️ Mermaid Pearl Dive

## 📖 About The Game
Mermaid Pearl Dive is a 2D underwater survival game. You play as a mermaid. Your goal is to collect pearls and diamonds. You must go to the water surface to breathe. You must also avoid dangerous sea creatures like sharks, jellyfish, and pufferfish!

There is also a "Mermaid Boutique" (Shop) in the game. You can use your diamonds to buy new items (Crop Top, Lipstick, Wings) for your mermaid.

## 🎮 How to Play
* **Move:** Use `Arrow Keys` or `W A S D` to swim.
* **Goal:** Collect pearls to pass the level.
* **Breath:** Watch your breath bar! Go to the top of the screen to get air.
* **Avoid:** Do not touch the enemies.
* **Shop:** Click the `⏸ Pause` button to open the shop.

## 💻 Computer Graphics (CG) Concepts Used
This game is made with pure JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas. No game engines were used. The project includes these CG concepts:

* **Sine Wave Physics:** Used for water surface waves, floating pearls, and seaweed movement.
* **Parallax Scrolling:** Background rocks move with the water current to create a feeling of depth.
* **Particle System:** Used for the trailing bubbles and the visual explosion effect when a pearl is collected.
* **Movement Physics (Damping):** Acceleration and friction are used for smooth swimming.
* **Dynamic Background:** The ocean linear gradient gets darker when you reach higher (deeper) levels.

## 🚀 Tech Stack
* HTML5 `<canvas>`
* CSS3
* Vanilla JavaScript
* LocalStorage API (for saving High Score, Diamonds, and Shop items)
